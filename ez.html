About Us

Established in 1982, Formerly Known as "Sto Niño Learning Center" Sto. Niño Institute of Science and Technology is a private educational institution in Quezon City, dedicated to nurturing young minds since 1982. We offer a holistic curriculum from Nursery and Kindergarten to Elementary and Junior High School, fostering academic excellence, character development, and a love for lifelong learning.

Our Programs

Nursery / Kinder

Early childhood education focused on play, discovery, and foundational skills

Elementary

Grades 1 to 6, with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, science, and values education.

Junior High School

Grades 7 to 10, preparing students for senior high and beyond with a strong science and technology foundation.
